Asked Questions

Where is Hotel Kailash Park located?

We are conveniently located near Saki Naka Metro Station, just a 2-minute walk away.

Is the hotel suitable for business travelers?

Yes, we’re located close to major business hubs and offer a peaceful stay ideal for work trips.

Do you have a restaurant?

Currently, we don’t have an in-house restaurant, but there are plenty of dining options nearby.

Is Wi-Fi available?

Yes, we offer free Wi-Fi to all our guests.

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Check-in is from 12:00 PM, and check-out is at 11:00 AM.

Do you allow early check-in or late check-out?

Early check-in or late check-out can be arranged based on room availability. Please check with our front desk.

Is the hotel family-friendly?

Absolutely! Our comfortable and safe environment is suitable for solo travelers, couples, and families alike.
